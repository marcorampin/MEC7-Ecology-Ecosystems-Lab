Estimation of Flavonoids

  • Aluminium chloride AlCl3
    • Formation of acid stable complexes with C-4 keto groups or C-3/C-5 hydroxyl groups.
    • Acid labile complexes with ortho-dihydroxyl groups in A or B ring
  • Complexes have a pink colour
  • Coomassie brilliant blue G-250
    • Creates a strong non-covalent complex with protein carboxylic group by Van der Waals force and amino group through electrostatic interactions
  • Becomes blue when binding with proteins
