Nitrogen regime

  • Symbiotic nitrogen fixation can increase tolerance to droughts
  • Some hypothesis are:
    • Increased plant root biomass and surface area
    • Stimulation of ABA production
    • Reduced transpiration rates and increased water-use efficiency
    • Enhanced plant’s antioxidant defense mechanisms
    • Slower and steady growth
    • Early signal of low water pressure
