Nitrogen Fixation:

Atmospheric N2 is unusable by plants

Some bacteria can convert N2 to useable NH3

Organisms utilise nitrogenase protein complex as a catalyst for the reduction of N2

They can break the triple covalent bond between the nitrogen atoms and attach three hydrogens

N2 + 8H+ + 8e- + 16 ATP → 2NH3 + H2 + 16 ADP + 16Pi
