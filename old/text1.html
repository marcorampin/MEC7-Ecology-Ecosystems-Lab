Background:

Medicago truncatula

  • Barrelclover
  • Native to the mediterranean region
  • It has a small diploid genome, is self fertilising and has rapid generation time.
  • Legumes can form a root nodule which can host nitrogen fixing symbionts.

Sinorhizobium meliloti

  • Gram-negative rhizosphere soil bacterium.
  • Forms a symbiotic relationship with legumes (mutualistic)
  • Symbiosis is enabled by nod genes
  • Fix nitrogen to ammonia and give excess to plant
